Overview

Purpose

Africa, a vast contains, has an immense tourist heritage, Côte d'Ivoire is part of it, a country located in western Africa, abounds with several tourist sites such as tropical forests and a French colonial heritage on the edge of the 'Atlantic. Abidjan, and Yamoussoukro are these two capitals Its modern sites include the Pyramid, a concrete work located, Saint Paul's Cathedral is an inclined architecture attached to a huge cross in Abidjan. In Yamoussoukro a basilica, larger than that of Italy. With a lake with caimans. The Banco National Park is a tropical forest reserve within which winding hiking trails. With beautiful beaches all along the southern coast.

Audience

This tourist adventure is intended for any category of person wishing to make new discoveries towards Africa, in particular the Ivory Coast.
